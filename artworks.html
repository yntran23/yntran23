Raku Untitled (2015)

Free-form ceramic sculpture with Raku technique

This is one of the first artwork I've made which made me want to persude an art career. Back when I was in Vietnam, art was something very fancy. Even though I liked to paint, to draw, but I've never had time to sit down and enjoy my painting time. I had to keep study and study to get good grades. When I graduated high school, I got in a university majoring in Marketing. Even when I transfered here, my major was Marketing at first. Taking art classes for GE had changed my thought. I enjoyed the time I spent making artworks; I enjoyed my works; and I enjoyed my time as an artist. Although I realized my passion for art a bit late, but later than never; I'm learning and I'll keep improving. The other side of the story, to make my life easier, I've always told my parents I'm Graphic Design major.
